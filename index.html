Transforming Communities with Reliable Water Solutions

For over a decade, our team of skilled drillers and engineers has been dedicated to bringing reliable water to homes, farms, and businesses across Zimbabwe — with precision, honesty, and craftsmanship on every site.

To become Zimbabwe's most trusted name in water access — making a properly drilled borehole the standard, not the exception, for every household and farm.

High-Performance Rotary Rigs

Our drilling rigs reach deep aquifers that others can't. We drill through hard rock, clay, and mixed geology to find water where it actually is — not just where it's easy.

02

Site Survey Before Every Drill

We conduct a full hydrogeological assessment before any rig touches the ground. This is how we maintain a 98% water strike rate across every site we work on.

03

Transparent, Per-Metre Pricing

You know the cost per metre before we start. No surprise invoices at the bottom of the borehole. Clear scope, clear numbers, every time.

Pump Service & Ongoing Support

We install the pump, certify the water, and stay on call. G-Tech clients get scheduled maintenance visits and priority response when something needs attention.
