Our Story

Built On
Experience & Trust.

G-Tech Engineering was founded with a clear mission: to deliver reliable borehole drilling and quality construction services to the people of Harare and beyond. What started as a small team of dedicated engineers has grown into one of Zimbabwe's most trusted names in groundwater solutions and building construction.

Operating from our base at 8 Williams Way, New Adbennie, Harare, we have completed over 200 projects across residential, commercial and agricultural sectors — always putting our clients first and delivering results that last.

Our team combines deep local knowledge of Zimbabwe's geology with modern drilling technology, giving us the ability to reach reliable water sources where others cannot. We take pride in every project — from a single household borehole to a full commercial construction build.
