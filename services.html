Our Services

From borehole drilling to full construction builds — G-Tech Engineering delivers comprehensive engineering solutions across Zimbabwe.

SERVICES
01
Core Service
01

Borehole Drilling

Our flagship service. Using advanced rotary and percussion drilling rigs, G-Tech Engineering reaches reliable groundwater aquifers at depths up to 100m+, providing sustainable water sources for homes, farms, and businesses across Zimbabwe.

  • Site assessment and geological survey before drilling
  • Rotary and cable percussion drilling methods available
  • PVC casing and screen installation
  • Borehole yield testing and water quality analysis
  • Completion report provided for every project

Pump Installation

A borehole is only as good as its pump. G-Tech supplies, installs and commissions submersible and surface pumps from leading brands — ensuring reliable, efficient water delivery from your borehole to your storage tank or direct supply line.

  • Submersible and surface pump supply and installation
  • Solar-powered pump systems available
  • Pump sizing and selection for your borehole yield
  • Electrical wiring and control panel installation
  • Ongoing maintenance contracts available

Structural Repair
